Hound Army Checkers


Both players begins the game with 12 pieces, placed in the three rows closest to their side of the board. The object of the game is to capture all of your opponent's checkers or leave your opponent with no more valid moves. Players can only move one of their standard pieces diagonally forward, one space at a time. (revise)
