Hound Army Checkers 


Welcome to Hound Army Checkers! Commander Eris of the Cat Army is
up to no good and is plotting an invasion of Hound Kingdom!

Your mission as a Commanding Officer of the Hound Army is to defeat
the Cat Army. Your hound troops are represented by the white pieces
on the checkerboard, while your opponent's cat troops are
represented by the black pieces.

You and your opponent will begin the game with 12 troops each. In
order to defeat the Cat Army, you must capture all of your
opponent's troops or trap them so they have no valid maneuvers left.
You can only move one of your standard pieces at a time, and only
diagonally forward by one space. To help with your strategy, hints
for available moves will be highlighted in yellow on the board.

If one of your troops manages to successfully infiltrate the Cat
Army and reaches your opponent's end of the board, their rank
will be promoted and they will be able to move forward as well
as backward on the board. Beware that the same rule holds true
for Cat Army troops that infiltrate your ranks as well!

Best of luck to you, Officer! The fate of Hound Kingdom rests
in your paws!
